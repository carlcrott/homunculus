The Product:

An open-source organism.

This strain will liberate biologics manufacturing from technical debt. We will evolve an established human line into a robust secretory organism optimized for manufacture. Once developed, we will offer production services to get clients through difficult scaling and cGMP validation steps.

The Plan:

To develop the organism for manufacture of Herceptin and Rituxan, the first two biologics which will be coming off-patent soon. Our conservative estimate of this market is 1.4 million patients/year worldwide. The pharmaceutical industry is revolutionizing as biologics are becoming a dominant paradigm in treatment. The ease of copying, sharing, and iterating will change attitudes towards patenting IP, like it did for open-source software. The end result is going to be better, cheaper drugs that reach more people. It’s our plan to be a big player in this new pharmaceutical landscape.
